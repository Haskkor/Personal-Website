Who am I?

I'm a french soon to be kiwi full-stack developer. I'm 31 years old, and I'm currently living near Toulouse, France with my wife and two children. I'm pretty much of a bookworm, and particularly enjoy mangas and fantasy novels. I also love all kinds of games. Role playing games, card games, board games video games...you name it and I'm in! Last but not least, I love sports. I train every morning, 6 days a week, fitness, gym and cardio workouts.

I use to be a boilermaker in aeronautics. I hammered metal sheets for 7 years but I always knew it wasn't the right place for me. So I get my feet back in shool and took evening classes for 2 years to obtain my High School degree. I always had a thing with computers so I decided to take a shoot and try to make my way in this domain. 6 years later, here I am with my Master of Science in Informatics! During those years I've been a Networks administrator, a Systems administrator and a full-stack developer so I can say that I'm sure of what I want to do for a living.
