Profile

Who am I?

I'm a french, kiwi wannabe full-stack developer. I'm 31 years old, and I'm currently living near Toulouse, France with my wife and two children. I'm pretty much of a bookworm, and particularly enjoy mangas and fantasy novels. I also love all kinds of games. Role playing games, card games, board games video games...you name it and I'm in! Last but not least, I love sport. I train every morning, 6 days a week, fitness, gym and cardio workouts.

I use to be a boilermaker in aeronautics. I hammered metal sheets for 7 years but I always knew it wasn't the right place for me. So I get my feet back in shool and took evening classes for 2 years to obtain my High School degree. I always had a thing with computers so I decided to take a shoot at it and try to make my way in this domain. I keep the evening class going for 3 more years in the university to obtain my Technology University degree in Informatics. In the same time I got my Technical Degree in Informatics and had my first work experience in this field. Finally, I went to the prestigious SUPINFO International Unversity and 3 years later, here I am with my Master of Science in Informatics!

My first job in my new favorite discipline was Networks administrator. University made me love development but I wasn't able to see what the job was like every day, so I took a step back and learned another job. After 3 years doing this, I jumped on an opportunity to switch for a Systems administrator / Python developer position in another company. Proving myself more than able as a developer, I've been offered a new job as a full-stack JEE / AngularJS developer for the next 2 years to build a skills management application. The mission eventually reaches its end and I'm currently on a JEE / GWT assignment. On a personal matter, I'm a huge fan of the Python language and I've had the opportunity to teach it to a class as SUPINFO Certified Teacher, which is my dearest experience so far.
